## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks merged into continuous sentences.
2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, and question marks added at audible pauses and grammatical completions. Capitalization at sentence starts.
3. **Captioner errors corrected silently** (common words, unambiguous proper nouns); logged below where notable.
4. **Tom's spoken slips bracketed** on first occurrence per section. (Few in this lecture.)
5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** Seventy-six paragraphs across approximately 52 minutes of audio.
6. **Student turns** labeled `**Student:**`. Most student questions are inaudible on the raw captions and marked `[inaudible]`.
7. **Preserved:** filler ("um," "uh," "you know," "okay," "I mean"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, repetitions, parenthetical asides.
