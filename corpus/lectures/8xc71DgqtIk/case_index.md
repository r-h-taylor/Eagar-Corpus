## Process / topic

### Maglay (MAGLAY) wide-strip-electrode overlay welding
- **Anchor:** `§1.p1`, `§1.p2`, `§2.p1`, `§2.p2`, `§3.p1`, `§3.p2`, `§4.p1`–`§4.p4`, `§5.p1`, `§5.p2`
- **canonical_cluster_id:** `PROPOSED: Kawasaki Steel Maglay wide-strip electroslag overlay welding process`
- **Frame in this film:** A wide-strip-electrode electroslag overlay process using electromagnetic coils on either side of the electrode to control weld-pool flow, prevent undercutting, and flatten the bead. Demonstrated at 150 mm electrode width; electrode range 25–250 mm, with claims extending to 300 mm.
- **Materials/systems:** stainless steel overlay on carbon steel pressure-vessel base plate; electroslag flux; electromagnetic field control.
- **Era:** Process likely developed late 1970s–early 1980s based on Kawasaki Steel's published work on strip electroslag overlay; film vintage unconfirmed.
