### MIT Lincoln Lab Quadulan radar dome bogie replacement
- **Anchor:** `§8.p2`–`§8.p4`
- **canonical_cluster_id:** "MIT Lincoln Lab Quadulan radar dome bogie replacement"
- **Frame in this lecture:** Tom's 1970s consulting case. ### Caltech rapid solidification metallic ribbon research
- **Anchor:** `§9.p1`–`§9.p5`
- **canonical_cluster_id:** "Caltech rapid solidification metallic ribbon research"
- **Frame in this lecture:** Pol Duwez's 1960s discovery: pouring iron-boron brazing-alloy melts onto a spinning copper wheel produces paper-thin ribbons at million-degree-per-second solidification rates, yielding amorphous metals with 500 ksi strength and stainless-grade corrosion resistance without chromium. DoD/NSF spent hundreds of millions in the 1970s–80s on this research line. Used as the prototype "great properties, can't make it bulk" cautionary case.
- **Materials/systems:** Iron-boron amorphous alloys; spinning copper wheel quench.
- **Era:** 1960s discovery; 1970s–1980s DoD/NSF funding push.
