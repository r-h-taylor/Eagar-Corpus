- §1 (intro): industry-specific framing, references Tom's prior session and the molasses-disaster pressure-vessel argument

### §3 — Titanium casting
- §3.p1: captioner rendered "wrought" as "rot." Corrected silently.
- §3.p4: $K_C$ rendered in math; the speaker's "we talked about that, KC, in the last class" preserved as a backward-reference because it carries content (it points to the fracture-toughness parameter, which the reader needs to identify).
