4. **Tom's spoken slips bracketed.** First occurrence in section: `slip [correction]`; subsequent: `[correction]`.

6. **Preserved in layer 2:** filler words, false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, repetitions, pocket-patting, item-locating. Speech artifacts are Tom's; removing them is editorial and belongs in layer 3.
