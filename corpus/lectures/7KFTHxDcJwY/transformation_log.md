*Lecture 5 (Codes and Standards, Summer 2012). Mechanical rules applied to the raw transcript to produce the cleaned-up middle layer.*

## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks merged into continuous sentences.
2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, and question marks added at audible pauses and grammatical completion.
3. **Captioner errors corrected silently** where unambiguous. Logged below.
4. **Tom's spoken slips bracketed** on first use per section, then bare brackets thereafter.
5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses** — 106 paragraphs across approximately 87 minutes of audio.
6. **Preserved as-is in layer 2:** filler words (um, uh, okay, you know, I mean), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, repetitions. These are Tom's speech artifacts.
