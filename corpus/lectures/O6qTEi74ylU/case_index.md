### Alloy 718 (Inconel 718)
- **Anchor:** `§5.p1`
- **Frame in this lecture:** Used as the principal example of an alloy developed for resistance to strain-age cracking, by virtue of being strengthened by gamma double prime (rather than gamma prime) precipitates and thus tolerating lower titanium and aluminum content.
- **Other lectures in corpus:** TBD. Likely a recurring technical reference in welding metallurgy sessions.

### Gamma prime / gamma double prime precipitation systems in nickel superalloys
- **Anchor:** `§4.p1`, `§4.p2`, `§5.p1`
- **Frame in this lecture:** The mechanism of strain-age cracking is attributed to rapid precipitation of gamma prime (Ni₃(Al,Ti)) and the cracking susceptibility is reduced by gamma double prime (Ni₃Nb) alloys with lower Ti and Al.
- **Note:** Technical reference rather than a teaching case.
