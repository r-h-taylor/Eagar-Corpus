# Transformation Log: Layer 1 → Layer 2

*Lecture 16 (Welding Metallurgy, Summer 2014). Mechanical rules applied to the raw transcript to produce the cleaned middle layer. This log records every category of change so layer 2 is reproducible from layer 1.*

## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks merged into continuous sentences. Sentence boundaries inserted at intonation/grammar boundaries.

2. **Sentence and clause punctuation inserted.** Periods, commas, question marks, dashes added per spoken rhythm. Capitalization at sentence starts and on proper nouns.

3. **Captioner errors corrected silently** for common-word misrecognitions and proper nouns (see per-section list below).

4. **Tom's spoken slips of memory bracketed** on first occurrence per section, then alone thereafter.

5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** Forty paragraphs across roughly 37 minutes of speech.

6. **Preserved as-is:** filler ("um", "uh", "you know"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, in-the-moment fumbles ("Lost my point or two", "I lost my plot"), pocket-patting / object-locating speech.
