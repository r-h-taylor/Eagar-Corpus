## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption lines (~3 sec each) merged into continuous sentences at true sentence boundaries.
2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, question marks added at audible pauses and grammatical completions. Capitalization at sentence starts.
3. **Captioner errors corrected silently** for common words and proper nouns where unambiguous. Logged below per occurrence.
4. **Tom's spoken slips bracketed.** First occurrence per section: `Coke [Koch]`, `Ford [Forward]` style. Subsequent occurrences in same section: bracketed form alone.
5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** 41 paragraphs covering ~45 minutes of speech.
6. **Preserved as-is in layer 2:** filler ("um", "uh", "I mean", "you know", "okay"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, pocket-patting, repetitions.
