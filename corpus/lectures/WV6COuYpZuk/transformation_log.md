# Transformation Log: Layer 1 → Layer 2

*Lecture 16 (WV6COuYpZuk), 3.371 Welding Metallurgy Spring 2014. Guest lecture by Mike Balfour on weld inspection. Mechanical rules applied to the raw transcript to produce the cleaned-up middle layer.*

## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks merged into continuous sentences. Where the captioner repeated a phrase across line breaks (a common artifact in this transcript), the duplicate is removed.

2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, question marks added where spoken pauses and grammatical completion indicate sentence breaks. Capitalization at sentence starts.

3. **Captioner errors corrected.** Misrecognitions corrected silently in layer 2; logged below.

4. **Speaker's spoken slips bracketed.** Mike's misnamings of his own name, technical terms get bracketed corrections on first use per section. The lecturer name appearing as "balfor" appears to be a captioner error not a self-slip; treated as captioner error and corrected.

5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** Sixty-six paragraphs across the ~37 minutes of lecture.

6. **Preserved as-is in layer 2:** filler words ("um," "uh," "you know"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, repetitions. All speech artifacts preserved.

7. **Speaker identification.** Most of the lecture is Mike Balfour. Prof. Eagar interjects a few times audibly. Student questions are marked **Student:**. Eagar interjections marked **Prof. Eagar:**.

## Captioner duplicate-line artifacts removed

The raw transcript contains many places where 2–4 words of audio are duplicated across consecutive timestamped lines (a known auto-caption artifact). These duplicates are silently removed. Examples:
- L42–43: "so quality so what is quality when you when / quality so what is quality when you hear the word" → "So what is quality? When you hear the word quality"
- L46–47: "in order for something to have / quality it has to be measurable it has" — duplicate of "it has" removed
- L82: "be the quality person or the" + "be the quality person or the" duplication folded
- L266–267: "testing those / those / testing those welds" → "testing those welds"
- Multiple similar fixes throughout.
