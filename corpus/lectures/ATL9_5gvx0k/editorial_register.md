## Conventions applied (per established catalog)

- Student turns: `**Student:**` label. Where Tom paraphrases the student's question and the student is briefly heard but not transcribed clearly, the question is attributed to Student and rendered in the form Tom evidently heard.
- Stage directions for physical artifacts: `*[Tom passes two tool samples around the room.]*`, `*[Tom holds up a yellow-paper sketch.]*`, `*[Tom distributes copies of an Ashby plot.]*`, `*[Tom puts up a photograph.]*`, `*[Tom looks for his periodic table.]*`. - Monetary values: `$5,000`, `$25 million`, `$110,000`, `$1,000x`. Some round rhetorical numbers spelled out per established catalog (e.g., "twenty years ahead", "ninety-seven percent").
- Backward-references ("we talked about that last time", "from Thursday") preserved when carrying content; cut where they are pure orientation.
