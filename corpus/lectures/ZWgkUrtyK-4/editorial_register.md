## Convention catalog flags

No new conventions required. All edits fall within the established catalog (filler removal, self-correction smoothing, stage directions, captioner garbling fixes, numerical handling).

One borderline case logged for editor review: **§8.p8 "five or ten percent" → "five or ten ppm"** is a substantive correction of a Tom slip that he corrects himself one sentence later. The pipeline default is to bracket Tom's slips (`percent [ppm]`); the silent correction was chosen because of the physical-impossibility test and the immediate self-correction. Editor may overrule.

## Decisions that are *not* permitted at layer 3

- Adding new sentences in Tom's voice (Feynman/Leighton/Sands could because Feynman was alive to ratify; we cannot).
- Reordering content across section boundaries.
- Merging cases Tom kept separate, or splitting cases he handled as one.
- Removing factual errors Tom made on tape. (Captioner errors are corrected silently; Tom's slips are bracketed; Tom's genuine misstatements are preserved and footnoted in layer 3 only when they would actively mislead a reader.)
