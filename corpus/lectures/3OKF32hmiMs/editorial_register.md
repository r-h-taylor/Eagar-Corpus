Most of layer 3's editorial work is on Alex's voice; Tom's interjections are treated with the same conventions as elsewhere in the corpus.*

## Conventions applied

Standard project conventions: filler removed; self-corrections smoothed; numerals per catalog §3; "Student:" attribution for student turns; stage directions in italicized brackets; backward references and pure discourse fillers cut. Tom's spoken slips bracketed on first occurrence per section.

A note on speaker attribution: in this lecture, the L2 already marks **Tom** and **Alex** turns explicitly, and L3 carries those forward. The "Student:" convention applies to audience questions.
