## §6. Measurement, the Seawolf, and the limits of what to look for [57:43]

`§6.p1` When I've been to places like Bath Iron Works or Newport News or Electric Boat, these fights occur all the time, and there's not a great relationship between SUPSHIP and the yard in most cases. `§6.p2` The size of that fillet weld is defined in the United States as the leg length. The problem is there are two leg lengths. `§6.p3` They had this little laser-based measurer.
