### GTD-111 superalloy patent dispute
- **Anchor:** `§2.p1`–`§2.p5`
- **canonical_cluster_id:** "GTD-111 superalloy patent dispute"
- **Frame in this section:** GE's mid-1990s heat-treatment patent grant on an alloy whose original patent had expired in the mid '70s, trapping operators who had already designed it into land-based turbines. Hot isostatic pressing as the rescue technology.
- **Materials/systems:** GTD-111 nickel-base superalloy; single-crystal turbine blades; hot isostatic pressing.
- **Era:** patent filed ~1980, granted mid-1990s; refurbishment route operational by ~2000.
