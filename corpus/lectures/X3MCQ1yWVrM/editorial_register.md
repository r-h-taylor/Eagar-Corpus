## Filler and discourse-marker handling

- "Okay" cut where mid-clause filler; preserved where it closes a teaching unit (e.g., §3.p2 "herding cats, as they call it" — final "okay" cut as redundant after the rhetorical close).
- "You know" cut throughout except where it invokes specific shared context.
- "I mean" cut throughout as discourse filler.
- Self-corrections smoothed silently where Tom said the same thing twice in different words.
