- **§3.p8.** The Caltech-founder identification (Arthur Amos Noyes) is inserted in brackets as silent factual aid. Editor should confirm: was Noyes the MIT president Tom meant? He was indeed an MIT chemistry professor and acting president (1907-1909) who later moved to Caltech. The match is confident but should be confirmed.

The story is Christensen as a Norwegian freedom fighter operating across the Swedish border; the direction of travel during the war years could have been either way at different times. Preserved as smoothed.
