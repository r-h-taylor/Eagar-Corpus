## Conventions applied (per catalog)

- Student turns labeled `**Student:**`; inaudible questions noted as stage directions.
- Self-corrections smoothed to corrected form.
- "Okay" preserved at rhetorical closes, cut as mid-clause filler.
- "You know" / "I mean" cut except where they invoke shared classroom context.
- Numerals: monetary values as digits ($300, $2,000); rhetorical numbers spelled out (a hundred thousand, four ping-pong balls, forty-billion-dollar contract); percentages as digits in technical contexts (40%, 5%, 98%); psi values as digits (55 psi, 5 psi, 100 psi).
- Captioner garbling fixed silently when unambiguous ("gnome" → "dome"; "were Street into" → "King Street Bridge" rendered cleanly).
- Backward-reference to prior session content (§1 King Street Bridge, the pound-saved figures in §3) preserved where it carries teaching content.

## Decisions that are *not* permitted at layer 3

- Reordering content across section boundaries.
- Merging cases Tom kept separate, or splitting cases he handled as one.
