## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks (~3–6 words per line) merged into continuous sentences.
2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, question marks, dashes added at audible pauses and grammatical breaks. Capitalization at sentence starts.
3. **Captioner errors corrected silently.** Misrecognitions of common words and proper nouns. Logged individually below.
4. **Tom's spoken slips of memory bracketed.** First occurrence per section: `Ford [Forward]` style; subsequent occurrences in same section: `[Forward]` alone. Multiple slips in this lecture, see below.
5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** Sixty paragraphs across ~50 minutes.
6. **Preserved as-is:** filler words, false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, pocket-patting and on-the-board drawing, repetitions, Tom's stage business.
