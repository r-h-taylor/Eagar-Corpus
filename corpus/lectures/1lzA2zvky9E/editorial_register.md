## Sectioning rationale

Ten sections were chosen based on natural teaching transitions. §1 is the opening "education as what remains" preamble; §2–§4 develop the structural-materials taxonomy (billion-ton club → functional materials → properties); §5 pivots to the socio-engineering frame; §6–§10 walk through individual externality categories (political, environmental, social/embargo, lead-as-historical-case, metallurgy/cultural). Tom's own pivot phrases ("Anybody have any questions on…", "Let me jump ahead to another externality") mark several section boundaries.
