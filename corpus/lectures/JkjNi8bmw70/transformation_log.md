## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks (typically 3–6 words per line) merged into continuous sentences.

2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, and question marks added at audible pauses and grammatical completions. Capitalization at sentence starts.

3. **Captioner errors corrected.** Misrecognitions of common words and proper nouns corrected silently in layer 2; logged below.

4. **Tom's spoken slips bracketed.** First occurrence per section: `original [correction]`. Subsequent: `[correction]`.

5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** 45 paragraphs covering ~44 minutes of speech.

6. **Preserved verbatim:** filler words ("um," "uh," "okay," "you know," "I mean"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, equipment fumbles ("what did I do, I just destroyed the automatic focus"), repetitions.
