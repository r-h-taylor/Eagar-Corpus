## Global rules applied

1. **Line-broken delivery joined.** Auto-caption line breaks (~3 seconds of speech each) merged into continuous sentences. Layer 2 honors only true sentence boundaries.
2. **Sentence boundaries inserted.** Periods, commas, question marks added where spoken pauses and grammatical completion indicate breaks. Capitalization at sentence starts.
3. **Captioner errors corrected.** Misrecognitions of common words and proper nouns corrected silently where unambiguous; logged below.
4. **Tom's spoken slips of memory bracketed.** First occurrence per section: `Whipple [Whittle]`; subsequent: `[Whittle]`.
5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses.** Thirty-five paragraphs spanning ~52 minutes of delivery.
6. **Preserved as-is in layer 2:** filler ("uh," "um," "okay," "you know," "I mean"), false starts, self-corrections, pocket-patting/object-hunting, repetitions, mid-sentence pivots.
