5. **Paragraph breaks at natural pauses** — typically 30s–2min of speech per paragraph. 55 paragraphs.

6. **Co-lecturer turns labeled** with `**Simone Belmar:**` and `**Steve Lyons:**` at paragraph start. Tom's return labeled `**Tom:**` where ambiguous after a co-lecturer turn.

7. **Preserved as-is:** filler ("um," "uh," "you know," "okay"), false starts, mid-sentence pivots, self-corrections, memory lapses ("I can't remember what I was talking about"), repetitions.
