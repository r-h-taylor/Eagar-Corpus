# Case Index — Lecture 29

*Citation format: layer 3 paragraph anchor (e.g. `§2.p5`). Click-through in the static site reveals the corresponding layer 2 passage with timestamp range.*

Lecture 29 is a continuation lecture in the welding metallurgy series. It opens with a baseball-bat aside, pivots to a compact treatment of magnesium welding (and its near-impossibility), and returns to aluminum alloys, developing liquation cracking in the heat-treatable 2xxx/6xxx/7xxx series. The cases are mostly framing examples rather than developed forensic units.
