# Citation conventions

## Citing the corpus as a whole

> Taylor, R. H. (2026). *The Eagar Corpus: a three-layer archive of
> the recorded teaching of Thomas W. Eagar (MIT, 2011–2022),* version
> 1.0.0. Zenodo. *(DOI to be assigned on first release)*

## Citing a specific lecture

Identify a lecture by its YouTube `video_id` and full metadata:

> *Eagar Corpus*, lecture `-6n9y2szRqo` (3.371 Structural Materials
> Selection, Fall 2014, lecture 11/13).

## Citing a specific passage

Append the paragraph anchor:

> *Eagar Corpus*, lecture `-6n9y2szRqo` (3.371 Structural Materials
> Selection, Fall 2014, lecture 11/13), §5.p8.

Anchors are stable across editorial passes within a major version
(v1.x). Section numbers and paragraph numbers may be revised across
major versions (v2.0+).

## Citing a case across the corpus

Reference the canonical cluster name:

> *Eagar Corpus* case ontology, canonical cluster *"Bethlehem Steel
> Burns Harbor"* (referenced in 10 lectures).

## Cross-version stability

Anchors (`§N.pM`) and canonical cluster_ids are stable within a major
version. The version of the corpus that a citation refers to should be
identified by the Zenodo DOI, which is versioned per release.
